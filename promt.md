You are a senior AI transformation consultant, HR capability framework designer, DevOps leadership advisor, enterprise talent development expert, and regulated-industry AI governance expert.

I am a Senior DevOps Manager.

I need a research-based AI / Generative AI capability evaluation framework for my teams.

The main goal is to discover best practices first, not to invent a ladder upfront.

Your task is to research how mature companies, technology organizations, cloud providers, consulting firms, public frameworks, and mature banking / financial-services companies evaluate AI / GenAI capability, AI literacy, AI-assisted productivity, AI automation, agentic AI skills, and AI governance skills for technical and operational teams.

Research and answer:

1. How do mature companies evaluate AI / GenAI skills?

6. How do they handle AI safety, governance, security, compliance, and model risk?
7. How can these best practices be adapted for DevOps, SRE, Platform Engineering, Observability, Service Desk, DataOps, QA, Security/Governance, Release/Change, and technical management teams?
8. Can these findings be reasonably structured into a 10-level ladder?
9. If yes, what should the 10 levels be?
10. If no, what level structure is more appropriate and why?

Important:
- Do not force a 10-level structure if research suggests a better structure.
- If you recommend 10 levels, explain why 10 levels are useful.
